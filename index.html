Hi, I am Karou.

I am a Ph.D. student at Mila and Polytechnique Montréal, advised by Amal Zouaq in the LAMA-WeST lab. I work on knowledge base question answering: teaching language models to turn a natural question into a precise SPARQL query, and to keep doing so when the wording changes.

More broadly, I want to give language models an external memory they can be held accountable to — non-parametric, up to date, and controllable — so that answers can be traced back to a source instead of hallucinated.
